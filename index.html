How to Use the Ouija Board

Ask your questions by typing them in the box or by speaking while holding the planchette.

  • Click and hold the planchette to speak a question.
  • Or type your question and press "Ask" or Enter.
  • The planchette will move to spell out the spirit's response.
  • Press ۞ to dismiss the current spirit and summon a new one.
  • All AI runs entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server.
